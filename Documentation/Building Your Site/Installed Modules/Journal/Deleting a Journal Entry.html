Deleting a Journal Entry

How to delete a Journal module.

  1. Click on your Display Name link - OR - Go to a Journal.
  2. Hover your mouse over the entry to be deleted. This displays a Delete  button to the right of the entry.
  3. Click the Delete  button. This will display the message "Are You Sure You Wish To Delete This Item?'
  4. Click the Yes button to confirm.
