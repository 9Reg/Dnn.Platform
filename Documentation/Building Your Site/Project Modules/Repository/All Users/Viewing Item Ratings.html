Viewing Item Ratings

How to view the rating of a Repository item. Ratings can be displayed as either a 5 star rating  or a 10 Dash rating . Note: Ratings may not be visible to all users.

  1. Locate the required item. The RATING score is typically displayed below each item.
  2. Mouse over the rating stars/dashes to view the rating %, and the total number of votes.
