Enabling Ratings on All Wiki Pages

How to enable users to add ratings to all Wiki pages.

  1. Select Wiki Configuration from the Wiki module actions menu.
  2. Expand the Rating Settings section.
  3. At Allow Page Ratings, to enable ratings. This will enable the remaining fields and check the "Enable on Existing" field.
  4. At Enable of Existing, to enable ratings on all new and existing pages - OR - to enable ratings on new pages only.
  5. Optional. At Default Rating Mode, to pre-select the Enable Page Ratings field when adding a new page.

  1. Click the Save button.
