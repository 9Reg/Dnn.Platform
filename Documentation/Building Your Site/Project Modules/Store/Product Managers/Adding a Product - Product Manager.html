Adding a Product (Product Manager)

How to add a product to the store. Note: If you wish to add customers who purchase this product to a security role, create the role before beginning this tutorial.

  1. On the Store Catalog, select Edit  >  Add New Product from the module actions menu.

  1. Complete Steps 3 onwards in "Adding a Product" or "Adding a Virtual Product". Once you have completed all steps, click on the name of the category that the product was added to view the newly added product in the Store Catalog.
