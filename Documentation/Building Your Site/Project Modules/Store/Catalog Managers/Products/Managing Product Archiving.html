Managing Product Archiving

How to archive a store product or remove it from the archive. Archived products are not displayed in the Store Catalog.

Tip: You can archive a product rather than delete it if you may want to restore at a later time. See "Managing Product Archiving"

  1. On the Store Admin page, click the Products link.
  2. At Category, select the category of the required product.
  3. Click the Edit link beside the product.

  1. At Archived, select from these options
    • to archive the product. This removes the product from the store and lists it as archived in the Product summary list of the Store Admin page.
    • to remove archiving. This displays the product in the Store Catalog.
  2. The product is now marked as archived.
