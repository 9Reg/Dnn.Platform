Unapproving Product Reviews

How to unapprove store product reviews and remove it from the Store Catalog. Unapproved reviews can still be managed and re-approved using the Reviews list of the Store Admin page.

  1. On the Store Admin page, click the Reviews link.
  2. At Status, select Approved Only.
