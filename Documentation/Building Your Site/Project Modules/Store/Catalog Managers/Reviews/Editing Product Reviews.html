Editing Product Reviews

How to edit the comments of store product reviews. Note: Ratings cannot be edited.

  1. On the Store Admin page, click the Reviews link.
  2. Optional. Filter reviews by Status, Category and/or Product as required. See "Filtering Product Reviews" 
  3. Click the Edit link beside the required review.

  1. In the Comments text box, edit the comments as required.
  3. Repeat Steps 2-5 to edit additional reviews.
