Editing your Forum Settings

How to manage your forum settings.

  1. Go to the Forum Administration page. See "Navigating to the Forum Admin Page"
  2. In the left-hand panel, select Forum > Edit Settings. This displays the User Forum Settings window.
  3. At Email Format, select HTML or Text.
  4. In the Threads Per Page text box, enter the number of threads to be displayed on one page at one time. The default setting is 10. Note: Authenticated Users can override this setting for themselves on the My Settings page.
  5. In the Posts Per Page text box, enter the number of posts to be displayed on one page at one time. The default setting is 5. Note: Authenticated Users can override this setting for themselves on the My Settings page.
  6. At Notify on Reply, if the users will receive notification of replies in threads they have posted in. Note: They can opt-out of notifications when they post - OR - to disable.
  7. At Enable "My Post" Notify, if the users will receive email notification of their own posts - OR - disable.
  8. At Moderator Emails, if the users will receive email notification of new posts awaiting moderation - OR - disable.
  9. At Clear All Read Status, click the Clear Read Status link to clear all read status for all forums and threads for this user.
