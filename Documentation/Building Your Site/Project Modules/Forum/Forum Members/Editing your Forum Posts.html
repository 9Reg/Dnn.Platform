Editing your Forum Posts

How to edit your own forum posts. Note: Post editing may be restricted to a number of minutes following the original posting. Login is required.

  1. View your threads. See "Viewing your Threads"
  2. Locate the required post and then open it by clicking on the [post title] link.
  3. Click the Edit link located to the right of the post title. This opens the Add/Edit Post page.
  4. Edit the subject or body or attach files as required. See "Adding a Forum Post (Advanced)"
  5. Click the Submit link. The post is immediately added to the forum and displayed to you.
