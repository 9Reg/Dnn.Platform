Filtering Threads from a Single Forum

How to filter the threads within a single forum.

  1. Navigate to the required Forum. See "Navigating to Forums & Forum Groups"
  2. At Display Thread From (located in the bottom right corner of the module), select a timeframe to filter thread by from these options: Today, Past Three Days, Past Week, Past Two Weeks, Past Month, Past Three Months, Past Year, All Days. This displays all matching threads.
