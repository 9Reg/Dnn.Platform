Managing Trackback Options

How to enable or disable trackback auto discovery for a single blog. This can be performed by bloggers on their own blog and by Administrators on any blogs.

  1. Click the Blog Settings link on the New_Blog - OR - Click the blog name on the Blog_List and then select Edit Blog Settings from the View_Blog module actions menu. This opens the Edit Blog page.
  2. Go to the Trackback Options section.
  3. At Trackback Auto Discovery (Client Mode), to enable - OR - to disable.
